Facts about Nagpur

  • Vice Capital
  • Orange City
  • Tiger Capital

The capital of Maharashtra may be Mumbai, but when it comes to being the vice capital of India, that title belongs to Nagpur.This title suggests that Nagpur plays a significant role in the administrative and political affairs of the country, serving as a hub for various governmental functions and responsibilities.

Read More

Nagpur, aptly known as the "Orange City," earned its nickname due to the flourishing orange cultivation in the region. Renowned for its succulent oranges, Nagpur takes pride in being a prominent hub for citrus production, contributing significantly to the country's agricultural landscape.

Read More

Nagpur, recognized as the "Tiger Capital," bears this title owing to its proximity to several prominent tiger reserves and national parks. Serving as a gateway to wildlife conservation, Nagpur plays a pivotal role in efforts to protect and preserve the majestic Bengal Tiger.
