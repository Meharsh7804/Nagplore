Nagpur's Wildlife

Nagpur boasts a diverse array of wildlife, including the iconic Bengal tiger, which finds refuge in nearby reserves like Pench and Tadoba. From leopards to exotic birds, the region's forests teem with life, offering visitors a glimpse into the vibrant ecosystems of central India.

Oriental Honey Buzzard

(Pernis ptilorhynchus)

The City bird of Nagpur is Oriental Honey Buzzard. It appears long-necked with a small head (resembling that of a pigeon), and soars on flat wings. The head lacks a strong supraciliary ridge giving it a very un-raptor-like facial appearance. It has a long tail and a short head crest. It is brown above and paler below. There is a dark throat stripe. Unusually for a large bird of prey, the sexes can be distinguished.

Nagpur's Vegetation

Nagpur's vegetation is predominantly characterized by dry deciduous forests, interspersed with grasslands and scrublands. Acacia, teak, bamboo, and tendu are among the prominent species found, fostering diverse ecosystems that support a wealth of wildlife in the region.

Oranges

(Citrus sinensis)

Nagpur oranges, grown in the heart of India, are famous for their exceptional sweetness and juicy pulp. They thrive in the fertile soils of central India, soaking up the region's abundant sunshine. With their vibrant hue and refreshing taste, Nagpur oranges are a favorite among citrus lovers both locally and globally. Enjoyed fresh or squeezed into delicious juices, they add a burst of flavor to any dish or drink.

Wildlife Santuaries & National Parks

Nagpur boasts a network of national parks and wildlife sanctuaries that are vital to preserving the region's biodiversity. These protected areas include lush forests teeming with diverse species of flora and fauna, offering visitors a chance to immerse themselves in the beauty of nature. From the enchanting melodies of birdsong to the majestic presence of elusive mammals, Nagpur's wildlife sanctuaries provide a haven for both wildlife and nature lovers alike.

Gorewada Zoo

(Gorewada Forest, Gorewada, Nagpur, Maharashtra, India)

Gorewada Wildlife Sanctuary, near Nagpur in Maharashtra, is a large area filled with diverse plants and animals. It's known for its beautiful scenery, thick forests, and calm lakes, making it a great place for people who love nature and animals. Visitors can enjoy activities like safari rides, walks in nature, and watching birds. Gorewada is important for protecting nature and animals, showing why it's essential to take care of our environment.
