City of Oranges

Nagpur is famous for its delicious oranges, known for their sweet taste. These oranges make the city's food taste amazing. From sweets like Santra Barfi to refreshing drinks like Nagpur Orange Juice, the oranges add a special flavor. Dishes like Saoji Cuisine and Tarri Poha also use oranges, giving them a unique taste. In Nagpur, people love their oranges and use them in many different dishes, making the city's food scene very special.

Assal Saoji

Nagpur's Saoji cuisine tantalizes with fiery flavors and aromatic spices, originating from the Saoji community of Maharashtra. Known for its rich, red gravy and bold use of ingredients like poppy seeds and bedgi chili, Saoji dishes, primarily non-vegetarian, offer a symphony of tastes. The cuisine's signature heat is balanced by creamy coconut and earthy sesame notes, creating a culinary adventure. Typically served with bhakri or rice, Saoji dishes have become a beloved tradition in Nagpur, drawing locals and tourists alike to savor its distinct flavors.

Nagpur's Street Food

Nagpur's street food scene is a vibrant tapestry of flavors and aromas, tempting passersby with its diverse offerings. From the tangy Tarri Poha to the spicy Saoji snacks, every corner boasts a culinary gem waiting to be discovered. Savory samosas and crunchy bhel puri tantalize taste buds, while sweet treats like Santra Barfi provide the perfect indulgence. With its bustling streets and mouthwatering delights, Nagpur's street food is a feast for the senses.
